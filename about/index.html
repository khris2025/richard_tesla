Binary bonuses are paid on the volume invested in your weakest leg. It doesnt rely on the number of people, but rather how much Bitcoin each member has in the trading pool. Now imagine as your team grows, and the amount of Bitcoin in each leg increasesso too will your weekly Binary Bonus increase.
